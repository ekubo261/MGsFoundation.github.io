Manas Gupta's NGO


We are a nonprofit organisation with the goal of increasing literacy so that every kid can benefit from both educational and economic possibilities. Digital material may now be distributed "offline" and without constant internet access, even in the most remote locations, using inexpensive mobile devices. The global scalability of our content is effortless. We don't require previous exposure to English, knowledge of any other language, or adult supervision. Our immersive digital material is provided inside an engaging framework with a heavy emphasis on graphics and music and is designed for personalised guided learning. It targets the foundational skills necessary for reading and is based on instructional second language acquisition research.
